Arithmetic Operators in JavaScript

i) Addition (+)
The addition operator is used to add two numbers. It can also be used to concatenate two strings.

ii) Subtraction (-)
The subtraction operator is used to subtract two numbers.

iii) Multiplication (*)
The multiplication operator is used to multiply two numbers.

iv) Division (/)
The division operator is used to divide two numbers.

v) Modulus (%)
The modulus operator is used to find the remainder of two numbers.

vi) Increment (++)
The increment operator is used to increase the value of a number by 1.
